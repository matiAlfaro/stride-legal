# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 9, 2026**

Stride ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

When you use Stride, we collect and store the following data **on your behalf**:

- **Account information** — email address used to sign in
- **Body measurements** — weight, height, and other health metrics you log
- **Nutrition data** — meals, foods, and meal logs you record
- **Supplement and medication tracking** — supplements and medications you add
- **Progress photos** — photos you choose to upload
- **Health & fitness data** — exercise minutes, hydration, and other daily logs
- **Apple HealthKit data** — weight and exercise data synced from HealthKit (see Section 4)
- **App usage data** — anonymous crash reports and error logs via Sentry

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Stride app experience:

- Displaying your personal tracking history and progress
- Syncing your data across your devices via Supabase (cloud database)
- Generating progress summaries and analytics within the app
- Diagnosing bugs and crashes to improve app stability

**We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties.**

---

## 3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using [Supabase](https://supabase.com), a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Supabase infrastructure is hosted on AWS.

---

## 4. Apple HealthKit

Stride reads and writes the following HealthKit data types:

- **Body Mass (weight)** — read and written when you log weight
- **Exercise Time** — read to sync activity to your daily log
- **Step Count** — read to track daily steps

HealthKit data is used solely to populate your personal tracking within the app. It is **never shared with third parties**, used for advertising, or disclosed to any external service beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

---

## 5. Crash Reporting

We use [Sentry](https://sentry.io) to collect anonymous crash reports and error logs in production builds. Sentry data does not include your email address or personally identifiable information beyond a random user identifier.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7).
