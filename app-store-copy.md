## English (U.S.)

**Promotional Text**

Track your bariatric journey in your language. Stride now supports English, Español, Português, Français, עברית, and العربية.

**Description**

Stride is your personal companion for bariatric surgery recovery. Whether you're preparing for surgery or navigating life after, Stride helps you stay on track with your protocol every day.

TRACK WHAT MATTERS
• Log meals, water, supplements, and medications
• Record your weight and body measurements
• Monitor daily completion and weekly progress
• Upload progress photos to see your transformation

FOLLOW YOUR PROTOCOL
• Personalized meal plans for each recovery phase
• Daily tasks based on your surgeon's guidelines
• Supplement and medication reminders
• Appointment tracking

UNDERSTAND YOUR PROGRESS
• Weekly summary with trends and streaks
• Achievement badges for consistency milestones
• Detailed nutrition breakdowns
• Export your data anytime

BUILT FOR YOUR JOURNEY
• Designed specifically for gastric sleeve and bariatric patients
• Works offline — your data is always available
• Privacy-first: your health data is never sold

Available in English, Español, Português, Français, עברית, and العربية.

**Keywords**

bariatric,gastric sleeve,weight loss surgery,post op,recovery,meal tracker,nutrition,supplement,hydration

Multi-language support is here.

Stride now supports English, Español, Português, Français, עברית (Hebrew), and العربية (Arabic) — with full right-to-left layout for Hebrew and Arabic users.

Choose your language during onboarding, or change it anytime in Settings → Language & Region.

Also in this update:

• Fixed: food slot picker not showing after logging from the meal library
